OmniRead

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

Overview

OmniRead provides serialized reading, account access, comments, rewards, ads, saved chapters, and optional digital purchases. This policy explains what information the app uses and why.

Information We Collect

We may collect account information such as email, username, guest account identifiers, login/session data, device fingerprint data used for guest sign-in and abuse prevention, reading activity, bookmarks, chapter progress, comments, reports, coin balance, purchase and subscription status, notification tokens, and app diagnostics.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide accounts, sync reading progress, unlock chapters, process rewards and purchases, prevent fraud and abuse, moderate comments, improve recommendations, send app notifications, provide support, and comply with legal or platform requirements.

Ads And Third Parties

OmniRead uses Google AdMob for ads and rewarded ads. Google and its services may receive advertising identifiers, device information, ad interaction data, and related diagnostics according to Google's policies. Google Play Billing is used for Android digital purchases and subscriptions.

Data Sharing

We share data with service providers only as needed to operate the app, including hosting, analytics, ads, push notifications, payment verification, fraud prevention, and support. We do not sell personal information.

Data Retention

We retain account and activity data while your account is active or as needed for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, and purchase records. Account deletion requests are scheduled for deletion with a 30-day purge window.

Your Choices

You can delete your account in the app where available or follow the instructions on the account deletion page. You can also control ad personalization and notification permissions through your device or Google settings.

Children

OmniRead is intended for teen and older audiences and is not directed to children.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@omniread.app.
