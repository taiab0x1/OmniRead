In-App Deletion

Sign in to OmniRead, open your profile or account settings, and choose the delete account option when available. The backend supports authenticated account deletion and schedules the account for purge.

Email Request

If you cannot access the app, email imtonski@gmail.com from the email address connected to your account. Include your OmniRead user ID if you have it.

What Gets Deleted

Account profile, sessions, reading activity, bookmarks, comments connected to your account, notification tokens, and app account data are scheduled for deletion or anonymization where required for safety, fraud prevention, purchase records, and legal compliance.

Deletion Timeline

Deletion is scheduled with a 30-day purge window. Some transaction, security, and compliance records may be retained where legally or operationally required.
